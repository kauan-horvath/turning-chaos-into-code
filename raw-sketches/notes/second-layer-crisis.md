## Refactoring | Modularization | Git Implementation

While refactoring is exhausting due to the lack of immediate reward, I cannot rely on the fun of creating messy blocks **eternally**.

### Creating without planning

**Programming without** a plan feels fun because we face no resistance. However, it progressively creates such a big mess that it becomes **unscalable**. I must **pursue** the future freedom of **scalable** projects.

* **70/30 Rule** - Keep 70% of the work challenging and structured, but also create a `sandbox.py` file to play around, experiment, and keep the pleasure of coding wildly.

* **Small Victories** - Noticing small **achievements** keeps the spirit alive. Remembering how to `git push` or handling an `except Exception as e` should be celebrated as an accomplishment, even if the big picture isn't working yet.

* **Accepting the Chaos** - Knowing nothing is the very basis of being a programmer. Being able to embrace this and slowly overcome the chaos in code is what **makes the difference**.

>It's step by step that we construct a path of a thousand kilometers.
